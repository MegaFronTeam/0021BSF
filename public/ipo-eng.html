How We Work

The project covers key aspects of the organization’s business, aligning them with the standards of public companies.

Depending on the company’s condition, changes may affect legal and organizational structure, financial reporting, corporate governance, information disclosure procedures, and investor relations.

Planning
  • Diagnosis of the company’s readiness for IPO
  • Strategy and transformation roadmap
Assessment
  • Independent audit support
  • Attainment of an investment rating
Restructuring and Transparency
  • Reorganization into a joint-stock company
  • Launch of IR and information disclosure services
Management Reform
  • Alignment of the Board of Directors with standards
  • Formation of key committees and the Board
Placement Preparation
  • Selection of a participant pool and platform
  • Development of issuance documentation, type, and size of placement
Roadshow
  • Development of roadshow strategy and marketing materials
  • Pre-marketing, roadshow support, and the start of securities trading

Timeline

The project cycle is estimated at approximately 12 months. Each stage significantly enhances business efficiency and partnersization.

Who It's For

The solution is suitable for mature companies with annual revenue exceeding 500 million rubles, a growth rate of over 25%, and leading positions in their segment in the Russian market.

Project Participants

The project is led by a partner at BSF Partners with at least 10 years of experience in investment banking, management of public companies, and organizing initial placements.
